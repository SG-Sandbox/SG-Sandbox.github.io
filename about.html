About Our Company

EchoBot Translation is revolutionizing communication in healthcare by eliminating language barriers between non-English speaking patients and care providers. Our integration of advanced AI-driven technology into a mobile robot ensures accurate, reliable and accessible translation. Starting in long-term care, EchoBot helps reduces isolation, confusion and miscommunication for aging residents, ensuring they receive care that truly represents their individual needs and preferences.
