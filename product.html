Step 1: Activate EchoBot

Press the “ON” button to begin the process.

Step 2: Speak the Phrase

Say the coaxing phrase into EchoBot’s microphone. Example: “Are you ready for your bath?”

Step 3: Speech Recognition

EchoBot converts your spoken phrase into text using its built-in recognition system.

Step 4: Language Detection & Translation

Our NLP code identifies the language and translates it to the target language.

Step 5: Text-to-Speech

The translated text is converted back into spoken words using TTS technology.

Step 6: Output

EchoBot speaks the translated phrase aloud through its speakers.
